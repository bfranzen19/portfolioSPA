#TheSkippyProject - how it all began

my father was always my world. when he got sick, my best friend bought me a nikon d3300 with a nikkor 18-55mm lens and told me: "i see a world where you travel around taking pictures and you're happy."

after my dad passed away on march 1, 2017, photography became an outlet for me. it allowed me to still see the beauty in this world despite his absence.


#TheSkippyProject & #MyDadsHat

the only thing i have of my dad's is a washington redskins hat (one of his closest friends was the redskins conditioning coach). since his passing, i have made it a point to take his hat to as many beautiful places as i can, which evolved into #TheSkippyProject.

since his death, #MyDadsHat has gone to the following countries, most of which were during the biggest trip of #TheSkippyProject at the end of 2017:

  • cancun, mexico
  • oslo, norway
  • stockholm, sweden
  • malmö, sweden
  • copenhagen, denmark
  • freetown christiania, denmark
  • munich, germany
  • innsbruck, austria
  • verona, italy
  • florence, italy
  • rome, italy
  • vatican city, italy
  • amsterdam, netherlands
  • brussels, belgium
  • london, england
  • reykjavík, iceland

the purpose of all of this is to live. i want to take my father's hat to all the wonderful places in the world and help remind myself that there is beauty in this world, even without him here.
